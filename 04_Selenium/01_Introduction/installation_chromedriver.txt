- Download Geckodriver from http://chromedriver.storage.googleapis.com/index.html
- Extract it

- Move it to one of the following options:
    - /usr/local/bin
    - One of the folders of sys.path
    - Add the folder where is located Geckodriver to the PATH:
        export PATH=$PATH:/path/to/directory/of/executable/downloaded/in/previous/step
